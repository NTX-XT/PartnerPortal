CROSSBEAM OVERVIEW

What is Crossbeam?

Crossbeam is a third-party tool that Nintex has made available to Certified and Premier partners as part of Nintex's continued promise to invest in our partner network.

Click here to watch a brief video that demonstrates the power of Crossbeam.

Crossbeam offers free learning courses that will give you the tools you need to quickly and securely begin the account matching process with Nintex.

How does it Work?

Quickly match accounts in which we have relationships and insights - resulting in more leads, opportunities, and customers for you.

Ensure secure account matching information which protects your customers and relationships.

Gain account data and insights on what your customers are showing interest in.
